#DIORAMA_DESCRIPTION
A computer generated landscape used by Delta Team for fighting simulations. The program is the home of the so-called Tutorial Man, a virtual entity programmed using movements and techniques from various different fighters.
This simulation can be directly connected with the brain of the user, allowing one to train their skills directly and feel like they are fighting for real.
Still, unlike in a classic movies from the '90s, dying here doesn't result in a permanent death in the real world.
